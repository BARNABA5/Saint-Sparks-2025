🌟 About Saint Carlo Acutis

“The Eucharist is my highway to Heaven.”

Blessed Carlo Acutis (1991–2006) was an Italian teenager who loved the Eucharist and used his talent in technology to spread the faith. From a young age, he attended daily Mass and had a deep devotion to Jesus. Using his computer skills, he created a website that documented Eucharistic miracles around the world. Though he lived only 15 years, Carlo’s joyful faith and love for the poor touched many lives. Before dying of leukemia, he offered his suffering for the Pope and the Church. He was beatified in 2020 and canonized in 2025, becoming known as the “patron of the internet.” His favorite quote remains: “The Eucharist is my highway to Heaven.”
