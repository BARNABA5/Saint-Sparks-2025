🌟 Blessed Carlo Acutis

“The Eucharist is my highway to Heaven.”

Blessed Carlo Acutis (1991–2006) was a young Italian teen who used his computer skills to spread love for the Eucharist and create an online catalog of Eucharistic miracles around the world. He lived a short but deeply faithful life, inspiring millions of young people to use the internet for good.
